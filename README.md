# Yugioh Card Maker

This project will let you create your own Yugioh Card just like the original Yugioh Card Game.

## How to use

### 1. Create an Account
  Create your own account by providing a valid Email, Username and Password.
  
### 2. Login your Account
  Login the account that you just created.
  
### 3. Create a Card
  Go to the respective page of the card that you want to create. From there, you can find the "Add Card" Button. Provide all the necessary information of the card to make it identical to the original Yugioh Card Game.
  
### 4. Download a Card 
  You can download the card that you just created clicking the "View" button on the Card Page.
***
### With that, you can now enjoy and share your card (memes) to your friends! 

#### _Note: If you can, please avoid creating a card that might offend a person, race, culture, nation, etc. ;)_
